MyWorkoutPlan

Monday: Push (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps)

Focus on pushing movements to target chest, shoulders, and triceps.

Dumbbell Bench Press

Targets: Chest, triceps

Sets/Reps: 4 sets of 8-10 reps

Rest: 60 seconds

Keep feet flat on the ground and lower the dumbbells to chest level. Avoid arching your back.

Incline Dumbbell Press

Targets: Upper chest, front shoulders, triceps

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 8-10 reps

Rest: 60 seconds

Maintain a slight arch in your back and avoid flaring elbows out too wide.

Overhead Dumbbell Press

Targets: Shoulders, triceps

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 8-10 reps

Rest: 60 seconds

Keep your core tight and press the dumbbells directly overhead without leaning back.

Cable Lateral Raise

Targets: Lateral deltoids

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Rest: 45 seconds

Maintain a slight bend in the elbows and lift to shoulder height only. Avoid swinging.

Triceps Dips (Bench or Machine)

Targets: Triceps, chest

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Rest: 45 seconds

Keep elbows close to your body during the dip. Avoid swinging your legs.

Tuesday: Pull (Back, Biceps)

Focus on pulling movements to target back and biceps.

Lat Pulldown (Wide Grip)

Targets: Lats, upper back, biceps

Sets/Reps: 4 sets of 8-10 reps

Rest: 60 seconds

Pull the bar to your upper chest without jerking. Keep your shoulders down.

Seated Cable Row

Targets: Mid-back, lats, rhomboids

Sets/Reps: 4 sets of 8-10 reps

Rest: 60 seconds

Keep your chest up and pull towards your navel. Avoid rounding your back.

Dumbbell Bent Over Row

Targets: Upper back, lats, biceps

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Rest: 60 seconds

Hinge at the hips with a flat back. Pull the dumbbells towards your waist.

Face Pulls (Cable Machine) OR Reverse Pec Deck (Optional)

Targets: Rear deltoids, upper back

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Rest: 45 seconds

Elbows high, squeeze shoulder blades together at the end of each rep.

Dumbbell Bicep Curl

Targets: Biceps brachii

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Rest: 45 seconds

Keep elbows at your sides and avoid swinging your body.

Wednesday: Legs

Focus on lower body strength and stability.

Dumbbell Goblet Squat

Targets: Quads, glutes, core

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Rest: 60 seconds

Chest up, knees out, maintain a neutral spine.

Leg Press (Machine)

Targets: Quads, hamstrings, glutes

Sets/Reps: 4 sets of 8-10 reps

Rest: 60 seconds

Avoid locking knees at the top. Control the movement.

Dumbbell Romanian Deadlift

Targets: Hamstrings, glutes

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Rest: 60 seconds

Hinge at the hips, flat back, and feel the stretch in hamstrings.

Leg Extension (Machine)

Targets: Quads

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Rest: 45 seconds

Align knees with the pivot point. Use controlled movements.

Seated Leg Curl (Machine)

Targets: Hamstrings

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Rest: 45 seconds

Keep hips down and avoid jerking the weight.

(Optional) Standing/Seated Calf Raise

Targets: Calves

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 15-20 reps

Rest: 30 seconds

Ensure full range of motion and pause at the top for maximum contraction.

Thursday: Shoulders & Arms

Balance delts and arms, maintain good posture.

Seated Dumbbell Shoulder Press

Targets: Front and lateral delts, triceps

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 8-10 reps

Rest: 60 seconds

Keep your back flat against the bench and avoid excessive arching.

Cable Lateral Raise

Targets: Lateral deltoids

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Rest: 30 seconds

Maintain a slight bend in the elbows and lift to shoulder height only.

Face Pulls (Cable Machine) OR Reverse Pec Deck (Optional)

Targets: Rear deltoids, upper back

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Rest: 45 seconds

Elbows high, squeeze shoulder blades together at the end of each rep.

Zottman Curl (Alternative to Dumbbell Hammer Curl)

Targets: Biceps brachii, forearms

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 8-10 reps

Rest: 45 seconds

Avoid swinging; rotate wrists at the top and lower with control.

Cable Triceps Kickback

Targets: Triceps (long head)

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Rest: 45 seconds

Keep your upper arm stationary; only extend the forearm back.

Friday: Core & V-Shape

Focus on abs and obliques to achieve a strong core and V-shaped waist.

Hanging Knee Raises (or Leg Raises)

Targets: Lower abs

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Rest: 30 seconds

Control the swing and engage your core throughout the movement.

Cable Woodchoppers

Targets: Obliques, rotational stability

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 10-12 reps per side

Rest: 45 seconds

Twist from your torso while keeping your hips stable. Avoid using your arms alone.

Plank (Hold)

Targets: Entire core

Sets/Reps: 3 sets, hold 30-60 seconds

Rest: 30 seconds

Maintain a neutral spine without letting your hips sag or pike.

Russian Twists (Dumbbell)

Targets: Obliques, abs

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 12-15 reps per side

Rest: 30 seconds

Rotate your shoulders and torso, not just your arms. Keep your back straight.

Reverse Crunch

Targets: Lower abs

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Rest: 30 seconds

Lift your hips gently with control, avoiding momentum.
