MyWorkoutPlan

Monday: Chest & Back

Focus on pushing and pulling movements.

Incline Dumbbell Press

Targets: Upper chest, front shoulders, triceps

Sets/Reps: 4 sets of 8-10 reps

Rest: 60 seconds

Keep shoulders back, no flared elbows.

Seated Cable Row

Targets: Mid-back, lats, rhomboids

Sets/Reps: 4 sets of 8-10 reps

Rest: 60 seconds

Chest up, pull towards navel.

Dumbbell Chest Fly OR Pec Deck (Optional)

Targets: Chest (inner/outer)

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Rest: 45 seconds

Arms slightly bent, controlled descent.

Lat Pulldown (Wide Grip)

Targets: Lats, upper back, biceps

Sets/Reps: 4 sets of 8-10 reps

Rest: 60 seconds

Pull bar to upper chest, no jerking.

Dumbbell Pullover

Targets: Lats, chest, serratus

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Rest: 60 seconds

Feel the stretch, keep core tight.

Tuesday: Biceps & Triceps

Arm isolation day. Mind-muscle connection is key.

Dumbbell Hammer Curl

Targets: Biceps (brachialis), forearms

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 8-10 reps

Rest: 45 seconds

No swinging, elbows at sides.

Cable Bicep Curl

Targets: Biceps brachii

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Rest: 45 seconds

Stand upright, no shrugged shoulders.

Overhead Dumbbell Triceps Extension

Targets: Long head of triceps

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Rest: 45 seconds

Keep elbows forward, full extension.

Cable Triceps Pushdown

Targets: Triceps

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Rest: 30 seconds

Elbows fixed, extend fully.

Incline Dumbbell Curl

Targets: Biceps peak

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 8-10 reps

Rest: 45 seconds

Elbows slightly behind torso, controlled.

Wednesday: Leg Day

Focus on lower body strength and stability.

Dumbbell Goblet Squat

Targets: Quads, glutes, core

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Rest: 60 seconds

Chest up, knees out.

Leg Press (Machine)

Targets: Quads, hamstrings, glutes

Sets/Reps: 4 sets of 8-10 reps

Rest: 60 seconds

No locking knees, controlled movement.

Dumbbell Romanian Deadlift

Targets: Hamstrings, glutes

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Rest: 60 seconds

Hinge at hips, flat back.

Leg Extension (Machine)

Targets: Quads

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Rest: 45 seconds

Align knees, smooth reps.

Seated Leg Curl (Machine)

Targets: Hamstrings

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Rest: 45 seconds

No jerking, hips down.

(Optional) Standing/Seated Calf Raise

Targets: Calves

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 15-20 reps

Rest: 30 seconds

Full range, pause at top.

Thursday: Shoulders & Arms

Balance delts and arms, maintain good posture.

Seated Dumbbell Shoulder Press

Targets: Front/lateral delts, triceps

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 8-10 reps

Rest: 60 seconds

Back flat, no excessive arch.

Cable Lateral Raise

Targets: Lateral delts

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Rest: 30 seconds

Lift to shoulder height, slight bend.

Face Pulls OR Reverse Pec Deck (Optional)

Targets: Rear delts, upper back

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Rest: 45 seconds

Elbows high, squeeze shoulder blades.

Dumbbell Hammer Curl (Light)

Targets: Biceps, forearms

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 8-10 reps

Rest: 45 seconds

No swinging, elbows stable.

Cable Triceps Kickback

Targets: Triceps

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Rest: 45 seconds

Hinge forward, elbow fixed.

Friday: Core & V-Shape

Focus on abs and obliques, aim for stability.

Hanging Knee Raises (or Leg Raises)

Targets: Lower abs

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Rest: 30 seconds

Control the swing, engage core.

Cable Woodchoppers

Targets: Obliques, rotational stability

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 10-12 reps per side

Rest: 45 seconds

Twist from torso, stable hips.

Plank (Hold)

Targets: Entire core

Sets/Reps: 3 sets, hold 30-60 seconds

Rest: 30 seconds

Neutral spine, no sagging.

Russian Twists (Dumbbell)

Targets: Obliques, abs

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 12-15 reps per side

Rest: 30 seconds

Rotate shoulders, no bouncing.

Reverse Crunch

Targets: Lower abs

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Rest: 30 seconds

Lift hips gently, controlled.
